PM KISAN SAMMAN NIDHI YOJANA

Kya hai: Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi ek kendriya yojana hai jisme chote aur seemant kisanon ko har saal 6000 rupaye ki arthik sahayata di jaati hai.

Kitna milta hai: Har saal 6000 rupaye, teen kiston mein - har 4 mahine mein 2000 rupaye seedha bank account mein.

Kaun apply kar sakta hai:
- Chote aur seemant kisan jo 2 hectare ya kam zameen ke maalik hain
- Bharat ke nagarik hone chahiye
- Parivaar mein pati, patni aur nabalig bachche included hain

Kaun apply NAHI kar sakta:
- Sansad, Vidhayak, ya koi bhi sarkaari pad par baitha vyakti
- 10,000 rupaye se zyada pension paane waale
- Income tax bharne waale kisan
- Doctor, engineer, CA jaisi professional degree wale

Kaise apply karein:
1. pmkisan.gov.in website par jaayein
2. Farmers Corner mein New Farmer Registration click karein
3. Aadhaar number, mobile number aur bank account details bharein
4. Apne gaon ke Patwari ya Lekhpal se verification karwaayein
5. Registration ke baad status check kar sakte hain

Zaroori documents:
- Aadhaar card
- Bank passbook
- Zameen ke kaagaz (Khasra/Khatauni)
- Mobile number

PRADHAN MANTRI FASAL BIMA YOJANA

Kya hai: Fasal Bima Yojana mein agar kisi natural aapda, keede-makode ya bimari se fasal kharab hoti hai to kisan ko muaavza milta hai.

Premium kitna lagta hai:
- Kharif fasal: sirf 2% premium
- Rabi fasal: sirf 1.5% premium
- Baagbaani fasal: sirf 5% premium
- Baaki premium sarkar bhrati hai

Kab milta hai muaavza:
- Baadh, sukha, aandhi, baarish se nuksan
- Keede ya bimari se 50% se zyada fasal barbad hone par
- Katai ke baad 14 din tak baarish se nuksan

Kaise apply karein:
1. Nazdiki bank ya insurance company se sampark karein
2. Online: pmfby.gov.in par jaayein
3. Aadhaar, bank details, zameen ke kaagaz, bowing certificate chahiye
4. Fasal ki bowing ke 10 din ke andar apply karein

KISAN CREDIT CARD (KCC)

Kya hai: Kisan Credit Card ek special bank card hai jisse kisan apni zaroorat ke hisaab se paisa nikal sakte hain - beej, khad, keetnashak kharidne ke liye.

Kitna loan milta hai: 1.6 lakh rupaye tak bina guarantee ke. Zyada zameen hai to zyada limit.

Byaaj dar: Sirf 4% sawaal (government 3% subsidy deti hai 7% mein se)

Kaun le sakta hai:
- Koi bhi kisan jo kheti karta ho
- Pashupalan ya machhli palan karne waale bhi
- Kisan producer organizations ke members

Kaise apply karein:
1. Nazdiki bank mein jaayein - SBI, PNB, ya koi bhi sarkari bank
2. KCC application form bharein
3. Zameen ke kaagaz, Aadhaar, photo dein
4. 2 hafte mein card mil jaata hai

PM KUSUM YOJANA

Kya hai: Kisan Urja Suraksha aur Utthaan Mahabhiyan - kisan apne khet mein solar pump lagwa sakte hain aur solar energy bech sakte hain.

Fayde:
- Solar pump par 90% subsidy (60% central + 30% state)
- Sirf 10% kisan ko dena hota hai
- Bijli bill zero ho jaata hai
- Zyada bijli grid ko bech sakte hain aur kamaai kar sakte hain

Kaise apply karein:
1. Apne rajya ki DISCOM website par jaayein
2. UP mein: upcl.org ya uppcl.org
3. Online form bharein aur 10% advance jama karein

SOIL HEALTH CARD YOJANA

Kya hai: Sarkar har 2 saal mein kisan ki mitti ki jaanch karke ek card deti hai jisme bataya jaata hai ki mitti mein kya kami hai aur kaunsi khad dalni chahiye.

Fayde:
- Bilkul muft mitti ki jaanch
- Sahi khad ka upyog - paisa bachta hai
- Fasal ka utpadan badhta hai

Kaise lein:
- Apne nazdiki Krishi Vigyan Kendra (KVK) jaayein
- Ya Block Agriculture Officer se sampark karein
- Mitti ka sample dein, 2 hafte mein card milega
